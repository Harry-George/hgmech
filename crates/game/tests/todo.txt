NOT TESTED — rules in rules.md with no corresponding test in this directory
===========================================================================

This file is the companion to the integration tests in crates/game/tests. Those
tests aim to cover *every* mechanical rule in rules.md. Each rule listed below is
NOT covered by a test, together with the reason. Reasons fall into three buckets:

  [FLAVOR]      Descriptive / force-building / fiction text with no mechanical
                effect to assert.
  [NOT-MODELED] A genuine game mechanic that the engine (crates/game) does not
                implement, so there is nothing to test. These are the engine's
                documented out-of-scope areas (facing/LOS geometry, physical
                attacks, terrain movement cost, water depth, the HT ability,
                formations/SPAs) plus a few smaller gaps noted below.
  [DIVERGES]    The engine deliberately implements the rule differently from the
                book; a faithful test would fail. Called out so the divergence is
                a known, documented choice rather than a silent gap.

If one of these mechanics is implemented later, move the rule out of this file
and add a real test for it.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
§1 The Unit Card
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Field glossary (Name, TP, PV, Role, Heat Scale layout, Critical-Hit track):
  [FLAVOR] data-dictionary text. The mechanically-meaningful fields are tested
  through their effects:
    * TMM .......... combat_modifiers.rs (target movement modifiers)
    * SZ ........... unit_card.rs (Size weight classes 1-4)
    * Skill ........ combat_modifiers.rs (base of the to-hit number)
    * Damage S/M/L . damage_and_crits.rs / combat_modifiers.rs
    * OV ........... heat_and_overheat.rs
    * Armor/Struct . damage_and_crits.rs
    * Specials ..... damage_and_crits.rs (CASE/CASEII/ENE), heat_and_overheat.rs (OVL)
- "Type (TP) is BM only" at introductory level: [FLAVOR] no other type exists.
- PV (Point Value) for force balancing: [NOT-MODELED] no force-building logic.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
§2 Sequence of Play
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Initiative ties are re-rolled: [DIVERGES] roll_initiative() breaks ties by
  player order (stable sort) instead of re-rolling. Tested behavior is "higher
  total acts last" (turn_structure.rs); the re-roll itself is not implemented.
- Combat damage "is recorded immediately but does not take effect until the End
  Phase": [DIVERGES] GameState::attack() applies damage and criticals
  immediately — there is no deferred-damage buffer. The one consequence of that
  rule that IS modeled and tested is "a unit destroyed this phase can still
  return fire": a unit killed this turn stays actionable in the Attack phase
  until the End Phase removes it (turn_structure.rs
  a_unit_killed_this_turn_can_still_return_fire, unit.rs destroyed_this_turn).
- Victory by scenario alternatives (breakthrough, capture, hold-position):
  [NOT-MODELED] only "destroy all enemy units" is implemented (tested).

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
§3 Setup
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Roll for force-list pick order, terrain placement by agreement, home-edge
  choice, entering/exiting from home edge, off-board start, deployment zone
  within 10" of the home edge, "any facing": [NOT-MODELED] the engine has no
  setup/deployment phase or board-edge concept; scenario.rs just hard-places
  units. None of this is enforced in testable logic.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
§4 Movement Phase
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
TESTED here: MV-budget ceiling, the always-available 2" minimum, the −2"/heat
slowdown, Standstill/Ground mode triggers, immobility (movement.rs).

NOT tested:
- Facing ("feet" direction; changes only in Movement; affects front/rear & arcs):
  [NOT-MODELED] no facing is stored on a unit.
- Jumping specifics — shortest path, pick any endpoint within jump MV, land any
  facing, may jump into water but not out of it, ignores terrain cost & level
  limits: [NOT-MODELED] Jump is only a MovementMode tag used for to-hit math;
  there is no jump pathing/terrain interaction.
- Jumping Move is not reduced by heat: [NOT-MODELED] movement_budget() subtracts
  the heat slowdown regardless of mode, and there is no separate jump-budget
  path. (The jumping-TMM half of that rule IS tested in combat_modifiers.rs.)
- Terrain & Movement Cost table (Clear/Rough/Woods/Water +costs, level change
  +2"/1", costs combine, 1"-deep water): [NOT-MODELED] movement is free up to the
  MV budget; per-tile terrain cost is explicitly out of scope.
- Level changes and the 2"-elevation-per-1"-travel prohibition: [NOT-MODELED]
  no elevation model.
- Stacking (pass through friendly, not enemy at same elevation; no two units end
  in the same space): [NOT-MODELED] no collision/occupancy checks.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
§5 Combat Phase — Weapon Attacks
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
TESTED here: range brackets & modifiers, the full to-hit summation, armor-then-
structure damage, the natural-12 crit, structure-damage crit, the crit table and
crit effects (combat_modifiers.rs, damage_and_crits.rs).

NOT tested:
- Step 1 Line of Sight — all of it (solid terrain <1/3 visible blocks; woods
  block at >=6" and otherwise add +1; base-to-base always have LOS; intervening
  units never block; partial cover between 1/3 and 2/3; water gives partial
  cover): [NOT-MODELED] there is no LOS geometry. Note: the +1 cover MODIFIER is
  tested (terrain_modifier), but cover is read from a rectangular zone the target
  stands in, not computed from a line of sight.
- Step 2 Firing Arc ("more than half the base outside the arc → no attack"):
  [NOT-MODELED] no facing/arc geometry.
- Base-to-base units cannot make weapon attacks (only physical): [NOT-MODELED]
  the engine allows a weapon attack at distance 0 (it classifies as Short range).
- Step 6 attack direction front/rear and the +1 damage rear bonus (and the
  corner-tie "target chooses"): [NOT-MODELED] no facing, so no rear hits.
- Minimal Damage (0*): roll 1D6, 4+ deals 1, a 0-result cannot trigger crits:
  [NOT-MODELED] damage values are plain u32; there is no 0* marker. (The plain
  "0 bracket deals 0 damage" case IS tested in combat_modifiers.rs.)
- HT#/#/# heat-on-hit at the damage step: [NOT-MODELED] see §10.
- Step 7 "if a rolled crit does not apply, deal +1 damage instead" (e.g. a Weapon
  Hit on a unit already at 0 damage): [NOT-MODELED] apply_critical_hit() silently
  no-ops such crits (e.g. reduced_by_one saturates) without the +1-damage
  fallback.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
§7 End Phase
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
TESTED here: heat resolution, shutdown, restart, per-turn reset (heat_and_
overheat.rs).
- "Apply all damage recorded during Combat at the End Phase, then remove
  destroyed units": [DIVERGES] damage is applied immediately during the Attack
  phase (see §2). Destroyed-unit removal is modeled as is_destroyed()/winner
  checks rather than physical removal from the unit list.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
§8 Heat & Overheating
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
TESTED here: overheat bonus at S/M, no Long bonus without OVL, OVL extends to
Long, overheat capped by the scale, heat paid at End Phase, cool-to-0 when not
firing, heat held when firing without overheat, shutdown at heat 4, restart,
heat→TN, heat→ground-move, heat≥2 → −1 TMM, shutdown −4 target mod, jumping TMM
heat-immune, engine-hit +1 heat on fire.

NOT tested:
- Choosing how many overheat points to spend (0…OV): [DIVERGES] resolve_attack()
  takes a bool and always spends the full OV (capped by the scale). The 0 case
  and the full-OV case are tested; intermediate point counts are not selectable.
- Every water-related heat rule — "−1 heat per overheat point used in water",
  "a unit in water using only 1 overheat: heat unchanged", "entering water ≥2"
  deep → −1 heat": [NOT-MODELED] water depth / being-in-water is not modeled for
  heat purposes.
- HT#/#/# and physical attacks may not be augmented by overheat: [NOT-MODELED]
  neither HT nor physical attacks exist.
- "Heat level applies starting the turn AFTER it is marked": partially implicit
  (heat only changes at the End Phase) but there is no dedicated test asserting
  the one-turn delay across a full turn cycle.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
§8 Heat (HT#/#/#) Special-Ability targeting
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Adds heat to the target's scale at End Phase on a hit; max 2 heat/target/turn;
  becomes normal damage if the target has no heat scale: [NOT-MODELED] the HT
  ability is not implemented (the card has no HT field).
